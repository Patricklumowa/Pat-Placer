PatPlacer

v1.0.0

Place pixel draft templates on WPlace. Upload an image, set your anchor point, and place all pixels as drafts.

Not on wplace.live

How to use:

  1. Go to wplace.live
  2. Click the P button in the toolbar
  3. Upload your template image
  4. Navigate to desired location
  5. Place first draft pixel as anchor
  6. Click "Place All Drafts"
